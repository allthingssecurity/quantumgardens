Quantum Gardens

Welcome to Quantum Gardens. Each carrot you pluck reveals a short quantum concept. Concepts pop up as a small cloud and get saved in your Book. Open the Book to review, flip pages, and learn at your paceâ€”all while dodging rivals.

Goal & Controls

  • Arrow keys - Move.
  • Z (or Y) - Hold to pluck a carrot (reveals a concept).
  • Q - Spend 1 carrot to push enemies away.
  • W - Spend 2 carrots to drop a decoy.
  • P - Open/close the Book (pauses the game).
